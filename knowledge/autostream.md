# AutoStream - Complete Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform providing automated video editing tools 
for content creators on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms.
Our AI-powered tools help creators save 80% of their editing time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?
A: Yes! You can upgrade anytime. You'll be charged the difference pro-rated.

Q: What platforms does AutoStream support?
A: AutoStream works for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, LinkedIn videos.

Q: Is there a limit on video length?
A: Basic plan: up to 15 minutes. Pro plan: unlimited video length.

Q: Can I cancel anytime?
A: Yes, cancel anytime from your dashboard. No hidden fees.
